About Ribbon Bloom Crafts

We are a small, passionate handmade gift business nestled in the heart of beautiful Sri Lanka.

At Ribbon Bloom Crafts, we believe that every gift should tell a story. Each box we create is carefully designed and handcrafted with love and attention to detail — because your loved ones deserve nothing but the best.

From choosing the finest ribbons and blooms to selecting quality items that delight and surprise, we put heart into every single creation. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, graduation, or just want to show someone you care — we're here to help you make it memorable.
